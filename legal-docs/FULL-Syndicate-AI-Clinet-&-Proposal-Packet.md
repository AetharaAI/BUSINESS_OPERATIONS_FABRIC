### 11. Term and Termination

Termination does not relieve Client of payment obligations already earned, accrued, incurred, or committed under an executed SOW.

### 12. Refund and Pilot Terms

### 15. Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with any executed SOWs, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, proposals, and understandings related to its subject matter.
