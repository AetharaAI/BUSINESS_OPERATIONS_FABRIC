# Syndicate AI Voice Agents
## Payment and Rollout Terms

To begin work, Client must complete the following:

1. Sign the Master Services Agreement
2. Sign the applicable Statement of Work
3. Pay the required deposit
4. Submit onboarding and routing information

## Default Payment Structure
- Deposit due at signing
- Remaining setup balance due before go-live
- Monthly fee begins at go-live
- Third-party costs billed separately unless included in writing

## Late Payments
Late or unpaid balances may delay onboarding, pause implementation, block go-live, or suspend live service.

## Accepted Payment Methods
- ACH
- Wire
- Card payment
- Invoice link, where available

## Rollout Sequence
- Signed agreement
- Paid deposit
- Onboarding intake
- Configuration
- Review / tuning
- Go-live
- Post-launch tuning
