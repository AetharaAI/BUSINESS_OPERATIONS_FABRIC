# Syndicate AI Voice Agents
## Client Onboarding Checklist

## Business Information
- Legal business name
- Public business name
- Primary contact
- Billing contact
- Phone number(s) to support
- Service area
- Website
- Business hours
- After-hours instructions

## Call Handling
- What types of calls should the agent handle?
- What types of calls should be escalated immediately?
- What should the agent never say?
- What services do you offer?
- What areas do you serve?
- Do you want appointment requests captured?
- Do you want emergency calls transferred live?
- Which number(s) should receive summaries?
- Which number(s) should receive escalations?

## Messaging / Summary
- SMS summary recipients
- Email summary recipients
- Preferred summary format
- Required intake fields
- Required qualifiers

## Approval
- Client confirms business rules are accurate
- Client confirms escalation contacts are correct
- Client confirms call handling assumptions are approved for rollout
